# BBMRI-LPC website launched

The Biobanking and Biomolecular Resources Research Infrastructure – Large Prospective Cohorts (BBMRI-LPC), one of the largest biobanking networks in Europe, launched in collaboration with Maelstrom-Research its new data portal [http://mineral.iarc.fr/mica/ ](http://mineral.iarc.fr/mica/) powered by the OBiBa Mica software. This first version of the BBRMRI-LPC catalog comprises 14 studies, 28 annotated datasets and over 18,000 variables.
